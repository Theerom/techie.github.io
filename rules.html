CLUB RULES AND REGULATIONS

Leadership and Meetings:

The Club President chairs all general meetings. In the absence of the President, the Vice President chairs the meetings. Club elections are to be overseen by the club patron. The Secretary is responsible for documenting the minutes of each meeting.

Membership and Participation:

Members are expected to be punctual and organized for every meeting. Cooperation among members is a key priority. Active participation in all club activities is mandatory. Members should be briefed on their roles and responsibilities before any activity or project.

Attendance and Punctuality:

Absenteeism, especially without prior apology, is prohibited. Breaching this rule more than three times may result in expulsion. Members must arrive for club meetings no later than five minutes after the start. Latecomers may be denied entry to that meeting.

Accountability and Responsibilities:

All assigned tasks must be completed within the given timeframe. Failure to do so may result in a penalty. The Club President has access to all club accounts, including the Gmail account and other social media platforms.

General Conduct:

Members are expected to conduct themselves in a respectful and professional manner. Membership fees must be paid promptly. Members must attend at least 75% of meetings and activities.

Amendments:

These rules and regulations may be amended by a majority vote of the membership.
