The purpose of The TechTribe is to foster a community of students interested in ICT, promote learning and innovation in technology, and provide a platform for skill development and networking.

About us

    This is a school club found in St.Janan Luwum kabalagala Secondary School. It began in 2022 with an aim of promoting awareness about ICT in the school. The club was founded by the ict teacher MR KINENE and was later on handed over to the current PRESIDENT OF THE CLUB (OGARA MARK RODNEY) who made sure that the club was lively and because of his ambitions the club still stands strong to date.
